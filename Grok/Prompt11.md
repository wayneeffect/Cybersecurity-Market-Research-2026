**Tips for choosing**:
- For **contract/staff augmentation and rapid needs** — Insight Global, TEKsystems, KORE1, or CyberSN often perform well due to scale and specialized databases.
- For **specialized technical or cleared roles** — Boutiques like CyberSN, Nexus IT Group, Redbud, or Scion provide deeper expertise.
- For **executive/CISO-level** — Christian & Timbers, Alta Associates, or Korn Ferry.
- Cybersecurity hiring emphasizes certifications (CISSP, CISM, etc.), clearances, and hands-on experience. Ask about vetting processes, time-to-fill (often 45 days or less for specialists), retention rates, and replacement guarantees.
- Check reviews on Clutch, Glassdoor, or industry lists, and verify alignment with your compliance needs (e.g., government, finance, healthcare).
