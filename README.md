# Cybersecurity Market Intelligence 📊

**Real-time insights and analysis of the global cybersecurity market** — one of the fastest-growing sectors in technology.

### Overview
The global cybersecurity market is currently valued at approximately **$220–270 billion** (2025–2026) and is projected to reach **$350–500 billion by 2030**, with some forecasts exceeding **$1 trillion in annual spending by 2031**.

**Key Highlights:**
- **CAGR**: 9–13.8% through 2030–2034
- **U.S. Job Growth**: 29% (much faster than average) for information security analysts
- **Global Talent Shortage**: Millions of unfilled positions
- **Fastest-Growing Segments**: Cloud Security, AI/ML Security, Zero Trust, Incident Response, and Threat Intelligence

### What This Repository Contains
- Latest market size data and forecasts from Fortune Business Insights, MarketsandMarkets, Cybersecurity Ventures, etc.
- Historical trends and projections (Excel + CSV datasets)
- Interactive visualizations and dashboards
- Job market analysis (entry → senior level, salaries, in-demand skills & certifications)
- Segment deep-dives (Cloud, AI Security, GRC, Endpoint, etc.)
- Regional analysis (North America dominance + emerging markets)
- Scripts for automated data scraping/updating (where legally permitted)

### Why This Matters
With escalating cyber threats, rapid cloud adoption, AI-powered attacks, and increasing regulatory pressure, cybersecurity has become a critical business and national security priority.
